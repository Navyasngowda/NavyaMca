MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS(MCA)
THE DEPARTMENT:

Department of MCA started in the year 2021,offering 2 years full time M.C.A.programme affiliated to VTU,Belgaum.

MCA is a postgraduate course that trains the students in the various aspects of computer programs, application software, computer architecture, operating systems and many more.

VISION:

To create Technical Professional who represent the union of best class room experience and practical exposure to face the global challenges.
